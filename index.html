Common Questions Answered

Are you a mobile detailing service?

Yes, I will come to you to perform the requested services. I’ll need access to water and power, as well as shade such as a garage or carport if performing paint work.

What services do you provide?

Please visit the Quotes page for an overview and price of the various services I provide. There you will also find specific details about the services.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

For suburban Melbourne, I do not expect you to pay a call-out fee. If you’re feeling generous, petrol money would be appreciated, but it is not required. For more rural destinations, I will discuss travel costs with you further if you are interested.

Are your prices negotiable?

Yes. If there are specific parts of a service you would not like performed, I am happy to reduce the price proportionately. If you believe you deserve a better deal, feel free to discuss with me further. The final price must be locked in and agreed upon.

Do you provide a guarantee?

I spend a great deal of time performing my services to perfection. If you are not 100% satisfied with the outcome of the service(s), I will rectify any issues you raise free of charge.

What is your availability?

I work full time as a software developer, so I am only available on weekends and public holidays. I can start as early as 6:00am and finish up as late as 8:00pm to suit your needs.
